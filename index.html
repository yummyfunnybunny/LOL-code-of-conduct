IN THE LEAGUE OF LEGENDS COMMUNITY, I WILL:

COMPETE TO WIN

I will compete to win and will support my teammates. I understand that actions to udermine that effort are harmful to the game's integrity and my community.

RESPECT MY TEAMMATES

I will respect my teammates and make every best effort to foster an environment where they can perform. I understand that respect builds trust, and that trust is needed for everyone to perform their best.

PROTECT MYSELF AND MY COMMUNITY

I will protect myself and others by making proper use of communication, reporting, and coordination systems. I will leverage these tools to help myself and my community be the best we can, in every game.

BE MY OWN LAST LINE OF DEFENSE

I will help Riot keep me safe. I will be responsible for my account, devices, personal information, and interactions with third parties.
